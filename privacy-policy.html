ZivaPet

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 27, 2026 · Last updated: May 27, 2026

ZivaPet is local-first. By default the app needs no account and your pet records stay on your device. Family sharing is optional — if you turn it on, you sign in with Apple and your household's pet data is uploaded to our backend so the family members you invite can see and update it. We don't run ads and we never sell your data.

1. Who we are

ZivaPet ("ZivaPet", "we", "us", or "our") provides a mobile application that helps people track the health records of their pets — vaccines, deworming, vet visits, medications, weight, and care reminders. This Privacy Policy explains how the ZivaPet app handles information.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Stored only on your device (default)

  • Pet and health data you enter — pet profiles, health records, weight entries, notes, microchip numbers, emergency info.
  • App settings — reminder preferences, weight unit, cached subscription status.

If you never enable family sharing, this data never leaves your device and we have no account for you and no access to it.

2.2 Collected only if you enable family sharing

  • Account — when you turn on sharing you authenticate with Sign in with Apple. We receive a user identifier and your email address (which may be Apple's private "hide my email" relay address), and a display name if you choose to provide one.
  • Your household's pet data — the pet profiles, health records, and weight entries in your household are uploaded to and stored on our backend so they can sync to other members.
  • Household & sharing data — your household, its members and roles, your pending email invitations (the email and a one-time invite token), and an activity log of who changed what (used for the in-app activity feed).
  • Subscription status — whether the household has an active Pro entitlement.

2.3 What we never collect

  • No advertising or tracking. ZivaPet contains no third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs, and does not use the device advertising identifier (IDFA).
  • No payment details. Card and billing data are handled by Apple; we never see or store them.

3. Notifications

Care reminders are scheduled as local notifications on each device, generated on-device from your data — they are not sent through our servers. You can grant or revoke notification permission in your device Settings and turn reminders on or off in the app. In a shared household, each member's device schedules its own reminders, and each member can mute their own.

4. Subscriptions and payments

ZivaPet Pro is an auto-renewable subscription sold through the Apple App Store. Purchases, billing, and renewals are handled by Apple. We use RevenueCat to manage and verify subscription state; RevenueCat receives a subscription identifier tied to your account but not your payment details. For family sharing, your household's Pro status is stored on our backend so invited members get Pro features without paying separately. Apple's handling of your purchase is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Family sharing & our backend

Family sharing is off by default. When you enable it, your household's pet data is uploaded to our backend, which is hosted by Supabase (a managed PostgreSQL platform). Access is restricted by row-level security so that only members of your household can read or change your household's data. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest by the hosting provider.

Please note: this data is not end-to-end encrypted. As the operator of the backend, ZivaPet is technically able to access stored data (for example to operate, debug, or comply with law). We do not sell it, use it for advertising, or share it except with the sub-processors listed below.

6. How information is used

  • Display your pets, records, and weight trends, and schedule local reminders.
  • Generate PDF health reports that you choose to export and share.
  • If you enable sharing: sync your household's data across members in real time, attribute changes in the activity feed, send email invitations, and unlock Pro for the household.

7. How information is shared (sub-processors)

We do not sell, rent, or share your data for anyone's marketing. Data leaves your device only when you initiate it (exporting a PDF, or enabling sharing). We rely on these sub-processors:

  • Apple — Sign in with Apple (authentication) and App Store payments.
  • Supabase — hosts the backend database and authentication for family sharing.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management and verification.
🔒 Our promise: local-first by default, no account unless you share, access limited to your household, never sold, export or delete anytime.

9. Your privacy rights

For data on your device, you can access, correct, export, or delete it directly in the app at any time. If you enable family sharing, we hold some personal data (your account and your household's pet data) on our backend; you can delete it via in-app account deletion, and — depending on where you live (e.g. under the GDPR or CCPA/CPRA) — you may have additional rights to access, correct, port, or erase personal data. Contact us with any such request.

10. Children's privacy

ZivaPet is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children under 13 (or the applicable age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Security

Data on your device is protected by the operating system's app sandbox. Shared data is transmitted over TLS, encrypted at rest by the hosting provider, and access-controlled by row-level security so only your household members can read it. It is not end-to-end encrypted. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we design ZivaPet to minimize the data that ever leaves your device.

12. International users

ZivaPet can be used worldwide. Device data stays in your possession. If you enable family sharing, your data is processed and stored by Supabase and may be hosted in regions outside your country; Apple and RevenueCat may likewise process data per their own policies.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, provide notice within the app. Continued use of ZivaPet after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
