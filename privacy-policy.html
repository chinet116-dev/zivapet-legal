Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 26, 2026 · Last updated: May 26, 2026

ZivaPet is built privacy-first. Your pet records live on your device, not on our servers. We don't require an account, we don't run ads, and we never sell your data.

1. Who we are

ZivaPet ("ZivaPet", "we", "us", or "our") provides a mobile application that helps people track the health records of their pets — vaccines, deworming, vet visits, medications, weight, and care reminders. This Privacy Policy explains how the ZivaPet app handles information.

2. Information we collect

We designed ZivaPet so that the app does not collect your personal information or your pets' information on our servers. Specifically:

  • Pet and health data you enter (pet profiles, health records, weight entries, notes, microchip numbers, emergency info) is stored locally on your device only. We do not receive, transmit, or have access to it.
  • App settings (reminder preferences, weight unit, subscription status) are also stored locally on your device.
  • No account is required to use ZivaPet. We do not ask for your name, email, or phone number to operate the app.
  • No analytics or tracking. ZivaPet does not include third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs, and does not use the device advertising identifier (IDFA).

3. Notifications

Care reminders are scheduled as local notifications on your device. They are generated on-device from the data you enter and are not sent through our servers. You can grant or revoke notification permission at any time in your device Settings, and you can turn reminders on or off within the app.

4. Subscriptions and payments

ZivaPet Pro is an auto-renewable subscription offered through the Apple App Store. Purchases, billing, and renewals are handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment card details. The app stores only a local flag indicating whether Pro features are unlocked. Apple's handling of your purchase is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Optional cloud backup & family sharing

ZivaPet may offer optional encrypted cloud backup and family sharing. These features are off by default. If you choose to enable them, your data is encrypted on your device (AES-256) before it leaves it, and synced through Apple's iCloud / CloudKit so that only you and the family members you invite can read it. We do not operate our own servers that can read your data, and we cannot decrypt your backups.

6. How information is used

Information you enter is used solely to provide app functionality on your device:

  • Display your pets, records, and weight trends.
  • Calculate due dates and schedule local reminders.
  • Generate PDF health reports that you choose to export and share.
  • Sync across your devices and chosen family members (only if you enable cloud features).

7. How information is shared

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or pet data with third parties for their marketing. Data leaves your device only when you initiate it — for example, when you export a PDF and share it with your vet, or enable family sharing. The only third party involved in normal operation is Apple, which processes your subscription.

🔒 Our promise: local-first storage, AES-256 encrypted backups, never sold, export your data anytime.

9. Your privacy rights

Because your data is stored on your own device and under your control, you can access, correct, export, or delete it directly within the app at any time. Depending on where you live (for example under the GDPR or CCPA/CPRA), you may have additional rights regarding personal data. Since we do not collect or hold your personal data on our servers, there is generally nothing for us to retrieve or erase on your behalf — but you may still contact us with any question or request.

10. Children's privacy

ZivaPet is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children under 13 (or the applicable age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Security

Data on your device is protected by the operating system's app sandbox and device-level protections. Optional cloud backups are encrypted on-device with AES-256 before upload. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we design ZivaPet to minimize the data that ever leaves your device.

12. International users

ZivaPet can be used worldwide. Because data is stored locally on your device, it remains in your possession. If you enable iCloud-based features, Apple may store your encrypted data on infrastructure in various regions per Apple's policies.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, provide notice within the app. Continued use of ZivaPet after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

14. Contact us

Questions about this Privacy Policy or your data? Contact us at privacy@zivapet.app.
